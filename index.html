Research Interests

Programming Systems (PL & SE) + Machine Learning (ML)

Currently, my research concerns combining empirical, neural, and symbolic techniques to build solutions that improve/aid software systems.

Particularly, I'm fascinated by how data-driven programs are changing how we develop, and maintain software -- Data Science, ML+SE, Program Analysis & Synthesis.
